REPIT - Re-Engineering Challenge
Event Date: 26-04-2024

Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education

Welcome to REPIT - Re-Engineering Challenge! A pivotal event hosted by Kare Open Source Society as part of Euphoria 2024: Techno-Management meets Sustainability, at Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education.
Team Specifications
  • A team must consist of a maximum of 4 members.
  • Every team must have a unique team name.
  • Each team should designate a team representative.
  • Inter-college and inter-specialization team members are allowed.
Prizes

Prize pool INR 15,000

  • Participation certificates for all participants
  • Free Coursera Guided Project licenses
  • Internship opportunities for the best performers
General Rules
  • Participants will be given a website to replicate.
  • They will then have the next 6 hours to replicate the website.
  • The websites will be viewed by the judging panel, and the top 3 websites will be selected.
  • Finalists will be scored on originality, understanding of functionality, and UI/UX.
