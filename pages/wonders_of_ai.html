Wonders of AI Hackathon
Event Date: 26-04-2024

Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education

Welcome to Wonders of AI Hackathon! A pivotal event hosted by KARE Open Source Society as part of Euphoria 2024: Techno-Management meets Sustainability, at Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education.
Team Specifications
  • A team must consist of a maximum of 5 members.
  • Every team must have a unique team name.
  • Each team should have a team representative.
  • Inter-college and inter-specialization team members are allowed.
Prizes

Prize pool INR 50,000

  • Participation certificates for all participants
  • Coursera Guided project licenses for free
  • Mentorship from IBM experts
  • Internship opportunity for best performers
General Rules
  • They will then have the next 24 hours to develop their solution.
  • The projects will be viewed by the judging panel, and the top 3 projects will be selected.
  • Finalists will be scored on originality, understanding of the problem statement, and the viability of the solution.
